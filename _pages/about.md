## Peng Yang / 杨鹏

I am an undergraduate student majoring in **Intelligent Vehicle Engineering (AI Future Technology Class)** at **Dalian University of Technology**.

My research interests include **medical image analysis, computer vision, deep learning, object detection, image generation, intelligent perception, and autonomous systems**. I am currently working on **AI-based meiotic spindle prediction in human oocytes from bright-field and Hoffman microscopy images**.

中文简介：

我是大连理工大学**智能车辆工程专业（人工智能未来技术班）**本科生。我的研究兴趣包括**医学图像分析、计算机视觉、深度学习、目标检测、图像生成、智能感知和自主系统**。目前主要参与**基于人工智能的明场图像人类卵母细胞纺锤体预测**研究。

## Current Focus / 当前重点

### AI-based Meiotic Spindle Prediction in Human Oocytes  
### 基于人工智能的明场图像人类卵母细胞纺锤体预测

This project focuses on meiotic spindle prediction in human oocytes from bright-field and Hoffman microscopy images. My work includes experimental design, model improvement, innovation proposal, video abstract preparation, patent and software copyright writing, and manuscript preparation.

本项目面向明场/霍夫曼显微图像中的人类卵母细胞纺锤体预测问题。我主要参与实验设计、模型改进、创新点提出、视频摘要制作、专利与软著撰写以及论文撰写。
