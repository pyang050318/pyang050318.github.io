This project focuses on AI-based meiotic spindle prediction in human oocytes from bright-field and Hoffman microscopy images.

My work includes experimental design, model improvement, innovation proposal, video abstract preparation, patent and software copyright writing, and manuscript preparation.

**Keywords:** medical image analysis, microscopy, image generation, object detection, clinical localization.
