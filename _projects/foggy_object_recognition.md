This course research project focused on object recognition for special vehicles in foggy environments.

As the team leader, I led the literature review, experiments, and writing of research reports on the comparison of defogging algorithms and object recognition in foggy weather.

**Outcome:** Awarded “Outstanding Micro-project” by the college.

**Keywords:** image defogging, object recognition, adverse environment perception.
